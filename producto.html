SO FRESH

Descripción breve del producto.Descripción breve del producto.

Hydrating serum with caffeine, vitamin C, vitaminc B3, cucumber, hemp and aloe vera.

Content 30ml.

A lightweight serum to refresh and rehydrate skin on a multi-depth level.


The formula of this facial serum contains tea tree, cucumber and extracts of hemp, algae and aloe vera. These natural ingredients help to soothe, hydrate and soften your skin, making it a versatile ally to keep your skin fresh and hydrated at any time of the day. This ultra-pure serum combines 3 types of hyaluronic acid and caffeine at varying molecular sizes with Vitamin B3 and Vitaminc C to offer lasting hydration and visible plumping of the skin at all levels of the dermis.
· Vegan
· Cruelty free
· Silicone free
· SLS free
· Dermatologist approved
SO FRESH is ideal for the daily care of oily and normal skin

· Caffeine - A natural skin brightener derived from wheat, has anti-inflammatory properties, so is particularly effective at treating redness and uneven skin tone caused by conditions such as antioxidation, pollution or tiredness.


· Hyaloronic Acid - Acts as a moisturising and supporting agent to keep it looking young. Moisturises, prevents wrinkles, provides radiance ...


· Vitamin B3 - Hydrates and heals dry and damaged skin by stabilising skin's barrier function and promoting the growth of stronger tissue.


· Vitamin C - Powerful antioxidant, brightening dull skin to targeting the effects of pollution and sun damage, fading pigmentation, targeting dark circles, and improving skin tone, vitamin C can benefit almost all skin types and concerns.
